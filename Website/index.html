If you'd like to follow John on Twitter, his username is @Ak_Tokyo_Duck.




Bio

John was born on February 18th, 1986 in Anchorage, Alaska. He lived in Anchorage until college, when he moved to Eugene, Oregon to study at the University of Oregon. While at the University of Oregon John studied Theatre Arts and Japanese Language. Throughout university he was in several university theatre productions and in 2007 he studied abroad in Japan.

Upon graduating John moved to Tokyo, Japan to teach English and American Culture to students who were preparing to study abroad. After teaching John became a Production Coordinator on the show "Pac-Man and the Ghostly Adventures." In addition to teaching and production, John also appeared on Japanese television several times.

John found improv while he was in Tokyo, and he became a member of Tokyo's only bilingual improv comedy troupe, The Pirates of Tokyo Bay. With the Pirates he performed in the Hong Kong International Improv Festival and The UCB Del Close Marathon in Manhattan in 2014.

In August 2014 John relocated back to the United States, and in January 2015 he moved to the San Francisco Bay Area. He is currently a Producer at Electronic Arts on the Pogo team. John plans to continue pursuing improv and comedy in San Francisco/LA in the future.
