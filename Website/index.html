About Me

I am a video game producer with over 7 years of production experience spanning across mobile, console, PC, and browser-based games as well as CGI animation and VFX.


I am currently based in the San Francisco Bay Area, and working as a producer at Electronic Arts on the POGO team.





Portfolio

Selected projects listed below. Full work experience available on LinkedIn.



Pac-Man and the Ghostly Adventures

I worked as a Production Coordinator on the fully CGI animated television series from 2013 to 2014. During my time on the series I worked on the animation and storyboarding teams. I managed the production of animation in selected epsiodes in the first and second seasons of the show. In addition to my work on animation and storyboard production I also interpreted direction between episode and animation directors with animation staff. I also attended voice recording and dubbing sessions with the Japanese voice over cast to ensure that humor and essential elements of the script were not lost in translation.



The Disney Afternoon Collection

I was the producer on The Disney Afternoon Collection. As producer I managed the production of this collection or retro titles for PS4, Xbox One, and Steam. I oversaw the P&L for this title and was responsible for approving milestons and ensuring the game's quality remained high. In addition to my duties overseeing the development of the game, I also reprsented the title to various media outlets and at Wondercon Anaheim in 2017.
